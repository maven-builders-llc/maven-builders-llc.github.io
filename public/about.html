Our Story

A small team that builds like it's our own name on the mailbox.

Maven Builders started with a simple frustration: building a new home shouldn't feel like a gamble. Too many families sign a contract, then spend a year in the dark — chasing updates, absorbing surprise costs, and hoping the finished house matches the promise.

We built Maven to work the other way. Before ground breaks, you get a fixed plan and an honest price. During the build, you get one point of contact and a walkthrough at every stage. And when we hand over the keys, you get a home built by local tradespeople we've trusted for years — not the cheapest crew available that week.

We're new as a company, but not to the work. Our team has decades of combined experience framing, finishing, and running builds — and every one of us signs our work.
